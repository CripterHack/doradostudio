We are born:
Architect juan carlos navarro (general director)
As a little boy I had always an intense creative spirit. Totally far from conventionalities, respecting the classical esthetics, I started designing and re-designing new things. That is how I found my passion in architecture while never narrowing myself to only designing and sculpturing habitable spaces, but any type of thing that could rise up the spirit.
“Life always has to taste something, something always different, something always vibrant that gives me vertigo during the process...”
juan carlos navaro
From here rises doradostudio by juan carlos navarro, design laboratory and studio.
Each of the architectural and interior design projects or any of the pieces exhibited in our showroom, are product of intense concept emotions . juan carlos Navarro - doradostudio has under its umbrella pieces of photography, paint, objects, furniture and clothing. All of them perfectly curated under the signature and design philosophy of doradostudio.
