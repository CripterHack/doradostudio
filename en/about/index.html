our mission :
to pedestalize the talent of young national designers, artists and artisans showing the best of the hand made and local materials furniture, objects, architectural finishes and materials and art pieces wise.

To be a gallery and showroom space that breaks stereotypes presenting avantgard and state of the art design proposals in architecture, interior design, furniture, clothing and lifestyle that wakes up soul emotions at first sight.

To work our profession always putting the client’s needs and budget as a priority. To better the esthetics of our environment respecting it and situating the architecture and design into a context of social responsibility.


Our vision:
To transcend as a voice of national design talent. To offer spaces and products full of integrity rescuing the identity of all intervened. To be pivots of style in each space or piece designed.
In few words, create to elevate the spirit.





Our experience:
With more than 20 years of combined experience of our team in Guadalajara, Los Angeles, San Antonio, Miami, Peru and New York now we begin a new journey in mexico city. in this new path, we will focus to provide original and down to earth proposals for our clients. We will push greatly our Mexican hand made labor creating a fusion in tradition with contemporary design. Rescuing the old fashion way of solving design problems befriending natural, simple and pure solutions and materials such as copper, wool, silver, clay, obsidian, volcanic stone, tropical woods, steel, leather, etc.

Doradostudio is composed of a peripheral vision consists of different views that defragmented trends and ideas in high-value concepts.
